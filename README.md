## Mission

My work focuses on establishing a unified reliability fabric across hardware, firmware, operating systems, cloud services, and multi-cloud CSP environments.  
The objective is consistent: eliminate drift, expose hidden causal structures, and convert reliability outcomes into measurable economic value.

This forms the foundation of the X-Series Reliability Architecture.

## Current Architecture Tracks

### XRAD – Extreme Reliability AI Debugger

### XENOS – Causal Boundary Guardian  
Ensures responsibility handoff integrity across silicon, board, firmware, OS, and service layers.
